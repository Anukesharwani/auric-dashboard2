Auric Sales Process — Abandoned Leads
1
Lead Identification & Categorization
Identify abandoned customers from the Abandoned Sheet. Categorize by product type, order value, and time since abandonment (0-7 days, 7-30 days, 30+ days).
2
Initial Contact & Engagement
Call customer using Agent Dialer. Use warm opening: "Hi [Name], I'm calling from Auric regarding your order of [Product]. I want to help you complete it safely."
3
Pain Point Discovery
Ask: "What prevented you from completing the order?" Listen to objections — price, delivery, product info, payment issues. Document in Tagging Sheet.
4
Solution Offering & Upsell
Address objection with: Discounts, bundle offers, free shipping, extended payment plans. Highlight product benefits. Offer alternative products if needed.
5
Tagging & Response
Tag response in Tagging Sheet: Positive, Call Back Later, Not Interested, Payment Failed, etc. Add notes on customer sentiment and next steps.
6
Order Completion
If positive: Guide through checkout via Intelliticks or direct link. Use Order Form for alternative payment. Confirm order in system.
7
Follow-up & Escalation
If "Call Back Later" — Schedule follow-up in Intelliticks. If issues arise → Use Escalation Form. For payment failures → Retry or mark for later.
Auric Sales Process — Retention (Hold for Payment)
1
Payment Issue Detection
Identify customers with payment blocks from Hold for Payment Sheet. Reason: Card declined, insufficient funds, gateway issues, etc.
2
Empathetic Contact
Call with: "Hi [Name], we had a payment issue on your order [ID]. I'm here to help resolve it quickly and securely."
3
Payment Method Update
Ask: "Can we update your card/payment method?" Provide Order Form link for alternative payment (net banking, UPI, COD).
4
Retry & Confirmation
Process payment with new method. Confirm order is live. Send order confirmation via email. Mark as resolved in Hold for Payment sheet.
